Our Story

Our Story

One day, 5 years ago, Diana was shopping one of her favorite brands online. Trying to figure out what size she should buy for new high-waisted skinny jeans, Diana started clicking through all the size charts and was immediately confused: the size measurements seemed totally contradictory.

This was born to empower you with the right to have every garment be uniquely tailored to your preferred fit — and to introduce a world where these size numbers and letters are truly meaningless.

Silicon Valley Meets Tailoring

Ever since we started this brand 5 years ago, our mission has focused on the forgotten art of tailoring. We have created a venture-backed technology-enabled platform with world-class engineers with the goal of revitalizing the tailoring community, composed mainly of marginalized individuals. We are proud to support our tailors, garment associates, and fit experts, who work tirelessly to bring the perfect fit to every shopper.
