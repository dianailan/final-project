RACHEL'S STORY

Ever since losing all that baby weight, Rachel finds that any pair of jeans which fit at her hips fall too baggy at the waist.
Based on her most comfortable pair of jeans from Madewell, Hemster created for Rachel her Comfy Skinny Jeans pattern. Any of Rachel’s skinny jeans, from now until forever, can be matched to this perfect fit.

“Instead of worrying about what size will fit both my hips and my waist, now I feel like I have the power to make any pair of jeans fit me, regardless of what the size on the tag says!”

Alo Yoga Leggings.
But make it Alisha

ALISHA'S STORY

At 5’4”, every pair of full-length leggings Alisha buys from Alo Yoga bunches at her ankles.
Alisha received a FitKit in the mail with her Alo Yoga online purchase. During a virtual appointment, a Hemster expert created the Ankle-Grazing Leggings pattern for Alisha and showed her how to use the Hemster stickers on her new legging to mark her desired length. Alisha mailed in her new legging (along with 4 other pairs of leggings she already had in her closet) with the provided shipping label. Hemster got to work on matching every single pair to her ideal length, and all 5 pairs came back to Alisha as if they were made just for her.
